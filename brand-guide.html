A fully capable, traveling expert. Cross-domain practice. The skill transfers, the rooms change.
Origin
A journeyman is someone who has completed their apprenticeship and travels to practice their craft in different places. The weld bead is the visual: joining different materials, different contexts, same skill.

05
On-Screen Rules
  1. Hard cuts. No fades, no dissolves.
  2. Screenshots sit on the wallpaper, not floating on black.
  3. Annotations use accent colors (torch blue / spark orange) on screenshots.
  4. The mark appears on the title card, end card, and lower third. Not on wallpaper or content frames.
  5. Text on screen uses Space Mono for labels, display face for titles.
  6. No more than two type sizes on any single frame.
