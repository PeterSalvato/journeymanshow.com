# Journeyman — Brand Style Guide

**Status:** Working draft. Evolving as the show takes shape.

---

## Identity

**Name:** Journeyman (not "Journeyman Show")
**Mark:** Weld bead pattern (lagged ladder weave) + wordmark. Asset 3.
**Concept:** A fully capable, traveling expert. Cross-domain practice. The skill transfers, the rooms change.

---

## Palette

### Foundation (steel/iron/concrete)

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Deep Iron | #1c1a18 | Primary background, title cards |
| Steel | #2d2a27 | Secondary background, wallpaper |
| Concrete | #44403a | Tertiary, borders, subtle dividers |
| Warm Gray | #6b6560 | Metadata text, secondary info |
| Light Gray | #b5aea6 | Body text on dark backgrounds |
| Cream | #eae7e0 | Primary text, mark, headlines |

### Accent (torch)

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Torch Blue | #3d8fb5 | Annotations, highlights, active states, section markers |
| Spark Orange | #cb6d2d | Accent, lower third bar, episode title highlight, bullet points |

Accents are used sparingly. Grays carry the frames. Blue and orange show up on lower thirds, episode titles, annotations on screenshots, rule lines, and dividers.

---

## Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weight | Use |
|------|----------|--------|-----|
| Display/Headlines | Calder Dark | normal | Episode titles, title card |
| Labels/Metadata | Space Mono | 400/700 | Lower thirds, timestamps, categories, URL |
| Body (if needed) | Rubik | 400/500 | Description text, episode info |

All caps for labels and categories. Mixed case for episode titles and body text.

---

## Graphics Package

### Title Card (1920 x 1080)
- Deep Iron background
- Mark + wordmark centered or top-left lockup
- Episode title in display type
- Episode number and category as label text
- Hard cut in, hard cut out. No fades.

### Lower Third (1920 x 1080, transparent PNG)
- Bottom-left position
- Dark bar with slight transparency
- "PETER // JOURNEYMAN" in Space Mono
- Accent color on the bar or as an underline
- Appears for 5-10 seconds, hard cut in/out

### End Card (1920 x 1080)
- Deep Iron background
- Mark small, centered or top-left
- Subscribe links: YouTube, Instagram, RSS
- "journeymanshow.com" in Space Mono
- Hard cut in

### Wallpaper (1920 x 1080)
- Steel or Concrete background
- Subtle noise/grain texture (not the mark as a pattern)
- Used behind screenshots, brand walk images, any evidence on screen
- The mat board that frames the content

---

## On-Screen Rules

- Hard cuts. No fades, no dissolves.
- Screenshots sit on the wallpaper, not floating on black.
- Annotations use accent colors (torch blue/spark orange) on screenshots.
- The mark does not appear on every frame. It appears on the title card, the end card, and the lower third. Not on wallpaper or content frames.
- Text on screen uses Space Mono for labels, display face for titles.
- No more than two type sizes on any single frame.
