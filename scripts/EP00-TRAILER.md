# EP00 — Trailer (60-90 seconds)

## Format
On camera. Direct to lens. No music bed. Just you.

## Script

I'm Peter Salvato. I'm a design engineer. I've been doing this for 27 years across construction, print, special education, enterprise software, and AI.

This show is called Journeyman, and it's about one thing: how the skill of making things transfers across domains. The tools change. The rooms change. The underlying structure doesn't.

Three kinds of episodes. Brand walks, where I pull up a company's web presence and read what's actually there. Connections, where I take two things from completely different worlds and show you the structure they share. And conversations with people I work with and learn from.

No hacks. No tips. No "five ways to optimize your whatever." This is practitioner-level thinking about design, brand, and creative work.

New episodes weekly. Subscribe wherever you listen.

## Notes

- No call to action beyond "subscribe"
